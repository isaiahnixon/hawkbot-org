Core Principles

Diversification

We strategically spread the fund across both assets and exchanges to minimize volatility and to prevent single points of failure.

Reliability

All of our data analysis and algorithmic trading scripts run on AWS to maximize uptime and reliability. See the AWS Well-Architected Framework.

Equity Buffers

We keep parts of the fund in US dollar and gold backed crypto assets (USDT, USDC, and PAXG to be precise) as buffers that allow us to respond quickly to market booms and dips.

Algorithmic Trading

We have algorithmic trading strategies built on hummingbot that execute orders at speeds and levels of precision that would be impossible to achieve manually.

Data Analysis

We collect and analyze data on fund performance, the wider market, as well as crypto development and social media activity. With machine learning and data visualization models we leverage this information for the continued development of the fund.
